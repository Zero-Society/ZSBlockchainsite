The Global Blockchain Ecosystem

A decentralized technology, a connected world

ZS Blockchain is building a global blockchain technology ecosystem, connecting grassroots innovators, communities, industry and government. We consult companies in blockchain implementation and facilitate strategic partnerships for M&A of blockchain technology, currently working with top companies internationally in Latin America. In conjunction, we are fostering future blockchain talent and changemakers through our blockchain education initiative. Our education curriculum is being implemented across cities in the U.S and countries abroad, accumulating into the initiative known as the Open Blockchain Forum.
